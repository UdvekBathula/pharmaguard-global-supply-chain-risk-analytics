# Problem Statement

Counterfeit and substandard medicines pose a significant threat to global public health and the pharmaceutical industry.

The objective of PharmaGuard is to analyze pharmaceutical supply chain data to identify high-risk routes, vulnerable transit nodes, and counterfeit drug patterns using SQL, Python, Pandas, NumPy, and Power BI.

## Objectives

- Detect counterfeit hotspots
- Identify risky transit nodes
- Analyze cold-chain failures
- Investigate price anomalies
- Build an interactive risk dashboard
